About Us

When we created this website, we really thought hard about whether ‘Only Top Deals’ (OTD), really required an ‘About Us’ Page. Because here, we are thinking, we have a baller name for our company that is as self-explanatory as a business name can be. Still uncertain? Don’t worry it’s for people like you that this page was created, so read on...

OTD is exactly what it sounds like! Top deals curated for any given product, e.g. earbuds. How did we decide what products deserve to be on our list? It’s simple - based on factors such as price, quality, worth, popularity, and reviews. OTD is truly based on products not brands – just because something is from ‘ABC’, it will not automatically receive a spot on our list. When exploring OTD, you will see we are not biased to any one company, brand or vendor. OTD is for customers seeking a true immersive shopping experience – for customers that care for the product over the logo they carry.

Please contact us with any feedback or suggestion you may have to improve OTD - we’d love to hear from you! Lastly, if you found value in OTD today, please do share it with your family and friends to help us grow.
